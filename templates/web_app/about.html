About BringMeHome

BringMeHome is the first website in Greece that aims to find a shelter for every homeless dog.

This Is Our Story

The number of stray dogs in Greece has been exponentially increasing. It is a crucial problem that needs to be tackled. The objective of our web application is two fold. Firstly, It provides a platform for reporting lost or found dogs. Moreover, it helps stray dogs find a potential family and a new sweet home. Users will be able to seek and report lost and found dogs across the country. Pet owners will be able to report their lost dogs. People who found a stray dog will have the opportunity to notify the community about the status of the dog. People who seek to adopt a dog will have the chance to find one through browsing our web application instead of buying one from a pet shop.

Who We Are.

Our team is comprised out of four tech guys. We love dogs and animals in general, and we think that by creating this platform we do our best to save stray dogs.

Our Mission.

We created BringMeHome in order to connect people to join forces against the problem of stray dogs. Our job is to provide shelter to homeless dogs and find dogs that got lost.

Our Vision.

We strive to help any dog that needs care and shelter. Our ultimate goal is to help every dog find a loving home. We dream of a Greece without any stray dogs.

Our Core Values.

We believe in combined community effort and hard work and we think we can raise awareness through our platform.
